Key observations

  • Stablecoin market structure remains concentration-led: leadership is still defined by a small set of large issuers/platforms; share shifts matter as much as aggregate market-cap growth.
  • Institutional relevance continues to rise: policy and bank-integration pathways increasingly frame stablecoins as payment and treasury infrastructure—not only crypto trading liquidity.
  • Industry headlines emphasize stablecoin policy & reserves: reserve and policy headlines can reprice which stablecoins issuers prioritize—platform concentration on this page may shift before aggregate market cap does.
  • Bank & payments integration: remittance and card-rail stories frame stablecoins as settlement infrastructure beyond exchange trading liquidity.
